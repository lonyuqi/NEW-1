roundsharp

advisor : Michail Semoglou

author : phoebe

The font is the university's first English font project. I created it with a grid. The text is heavy and the white space is compressed by black color blocks. Shape the font by its positive and negative Spaces. As for the details of the font, I changed the sharp corners to rounded corners in some places, making the whole font more durable and rich in details. Typefaces are designed to balance roundness and sharpness, to make them look interesting and technical.
