This project is an image narrative project. By analyzing some works of Austrian painter Schiller, we believe that his works present incisively and vividly without any hidden desire. The controversy about him is just like the personality theory put forward by Freud: society always evaluates his works from the perspective of "superego". So we made a video inspired by the Three Selves to explore the relationship between the three selves.
